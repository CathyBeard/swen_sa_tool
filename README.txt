Required Jar files are available under <jarfiles> folder.
Step 1: After loading the project into Eclipse/NetBeans right click "StockReporter" and Properties.
Step 2: Click "Java Build Path" and "Libraries" and click "Add JARs"
Step 3: Expand "StockReporter" and "jarfiles" and 
		select all the jars to be added to the build path and click "ok" and click "apply and close".
